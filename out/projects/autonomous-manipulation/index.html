This project focuses on developing methods for robots to autonomously manipulate objects in unstructured environments. Our approach combines perception, planning, and control to enable robust manipulation in real-world scenarios.

Research Goals

  1. Visual Perception: Develop robust visual perception systems for object recognition and pose estimation.
  2. Motion Planning: Create efficient motion planning algorithms for complex manipulation tasks.
  3. Learning from Demonstration: Enable robots to learn new skills from human demonstrations.
